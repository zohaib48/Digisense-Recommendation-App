Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 6, 2026

1. Introduction

Product Recommendations App ("we", "our", "us") is a Shopify application that provides smart product recommendations to merchants. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you install and use our app.

2. Information We Collect

When you install our app, we access and store the following data from your Shopify store:

  • Product catalog data: Product titles, descriptions, prices, images, tags, product types, variants, and vendor information.
  • Store information: Your store domain (e.g., your-store.myshopify.com).
  • App settings: Recommendation filter preferences you configure within the app.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any of the following:

  • Customer personal information (names, emails, addresses, phone numbers)
  • Order data or purchase history
  • Payment or financial information
  • Browsing behavior or analytics data
  • Any other personally identifiable information (PII)

4. How We Use Information

The product catalog data we store is used solely to:

  • Generate product recommendations based on size, price, category, and tags
  • Display recommended products on your storefront via theme app extensions
  • Keep our local database synchronized with your Shopify catalog via webhooks

5. Data Storage and Security

Product data is stored in a secure PostgreSQL database. Access tokens are stored encrypted and are only used to communicate with the Shopify Admin API on your behalf. We use industry-standard security practices including HMAC signature verification for all webhook communications.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties. Your product data is used exclusively to power recommendations within your own store.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

When you uninstall the app, all data associated with your store — including product data, settings, and session tokens — is automatically deleted from our systems. This process also occurs when Shopify sends a shop/redact GDPR webhook.

8. GDPR Compliance

We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable privacy laws. We handle the following Shopify mandatory webhooks:

  • Customer Data Request: Since we do not store customer PII, we acknowledge the request and confirm no data is held.
  • Customer Data Erasure: Since we do not store customer PII, we acknowledge the request and confirm no data needs deletion.
  • Shop Data Erasure: We delete all store data including products, settings, and sessions.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the data we hold about your store
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (also happens automatically on uninstall)
  • Withdraw consent by uninstalling the app

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
